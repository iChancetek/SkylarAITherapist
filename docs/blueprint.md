# **App Name**: Skylar: AI Voice Therapy

## Core Features:

- AI Therapy: AI-Powered Therapy: Provides voice-interactive therapy using a natural language model trained on evidence-based therapeutic techniques and the persona as defined. It serves as a 'tool' and can recognize emotional cues from speech to provide tailored support, adapting conversation as it proceeds. It will recognize interruptions to simulate a more natural conversational feel.
- Voice Interface: Real-time Voice Interaction: Uses real-time voice synthesis and transcription for full-duplex communication, allowing the user to interrupt and interact naturally. Relies on available Speech-to-text and Text-to-speech tools.
- Session Initialization: Emotional Check-in: Begins each session with a gentle emotional check-in, guiding the conversation based on the user's feelings. Leverages techniques like asking open-ended questions and reflective listening.
- Safety Protocols: Safety and Boundaries: Includes safety protocols for high-risk emotions, offering resources such as crisis hotline information and grounding exercises.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#D1B0FF) to promote calmness and relaxation, aligning with the therapeutic goals of the application.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) to ensure readability and a non-intrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Pale blue (#ADD8E6) to subtly highlight key interactive elements and provide a sense of ease and reassurance.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a balance of modern clarity and gentle warmth, suitable for a supportive therapeutic context.
- Use gentle, rounded icons in a minimalist style to represent various therapeutic exercises and tools.
- Maintain a clean and uncluttered interface with plenty of whitespace to minimize visual stress.
- Incorporate subtle, calming animations such as fade-ins and transitions to create a smooth and non-jarring user experience.
